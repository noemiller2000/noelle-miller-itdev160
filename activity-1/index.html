Animal Appreciation Post

Below, I will be talking about my favorite animal:

The Mantis Shrimp

They have some of the weirdest and coolest traits I've ever seen.
Here's a list of some of my favorites facts:

  • They come in a variety of vibrant colors (Just go ahead and google the Peacock Mantis Shrimp).
  • While the human eye can detect three color wavelengths, the Mantis Shrimp can detect twelve.
    • Try to imagine there are nine colors that you can't see but the Mantis Shrimp can. Pretty crazy, right?
  • There are two types of Mantis Shrimp, spearers and smashers.
    • The smasher type is definitely where the Mantis Shrimp shines, with punches that have the same acceleration as a 22 caliber bullet.
    • The speed and power of the punches actually cause vacuums and shockwaves to form underwater.

Probably best to end the list there, since I don't think we can get much better than tiny underwater explosions.
This concludes my mantis shrimp appreciation post.
